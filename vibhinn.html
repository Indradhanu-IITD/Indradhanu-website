ABOUT VIBHINN

Vibhinn is the LGBTQIA+ fest of IIT Delhi organised by Indradhanu, the LGBTQIA+ Collective of IIT Delhi. Vibhinn symbolises a community of diverse identities, thoughts and emotions that flourish in queer spaces. It aims to celebrate and unravel the human diversity that uniquely individualises us and yet connects us in humane ways.

Theme for the 3rd edition is "Repairing Recognition"
